Skills health check

The skills health check is a set of quizzes, questions and activities, designed to help you learn more about your skills, interests and motivations.

You can do up to 4 personal skills assessments, and 6 activity assessments to get a report. This report can help you to think about the types of jobs that might be right for you.

It will take you around 3 hours to complete all of the assessments.

You'll be able to save and download your report once you've completed at least one assessment. You can come back to it and do more assessments at any time.
