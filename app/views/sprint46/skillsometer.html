Match your skills to a career

Skillsometer can help you discover what jobs you might like to do in future, based on your responses to a short set of questions. You can use this as a starting point when exploring your career options, or inspire you to look at a new career path if you’re considering changing career.

The Skillsometer is simple to use. You will be presented with 3 cards at a time. Select the emoji that matches how you feel about the statement on the card. Repeat this for all the cards in the exercise. When you’ve completed the exercise, you will be given some recommendations based on your answers.

Next steps

Now that you've received some career suggestions, you might want to:

  • take a more in-depth skills assessment
  • explore careersbased on your Skillsometer recommendations

You can also speak to an adviser about your next steps by phone, email or webchat if you want more support with your career decisions.
