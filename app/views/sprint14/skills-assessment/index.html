Quick quiz

Take a 5 to 10 minute quiz to find out what groups of jobs, and particular job roles, might suit you.

You can save your assessment at any point and return to it later. You’ll get a summary of your results.

In depth assessment

Answer a set of questions to help you understand your work interests, motivations, what you're good at and how you like to work. You'll need 15 minutes to complete at least 1 of the sections and 1 hour to answer all 4.

Spending between 15 minutes to 2 hours answering additional questions will help you with creating your CV, completing a job application, or preparing for an interview. There are 6 additional sections to complete that assess your work-related skills, such as written communication and problem solving.

You will also get advice on how to improve your skills in areas that need attention.

You can save your progress and return to it later. You’ll get a report with your results.
